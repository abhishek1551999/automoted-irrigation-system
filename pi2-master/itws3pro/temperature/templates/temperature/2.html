Smart Irrigation

At first , User requests the project admin to see the condition of plants.
Project admin activates the sensors.

Soil Moisture Sensor:

It detects the moisture level of the soil and sends data to the microcontroller.
The microcontroller sends the data to cloud through blutoothmodule (or) Gps module.
It also checks the received data and if needed it activates actuatersand waters the plants.

Flame Sensor:

Farmers having Crops like wheat have fear that their crops catch fire.
Farmers having Crops like wheat have fear that their crops catch fire.
If any such incident happens then flame sensor detects the flame and alerts the farmers.

Temp and humidity sensor:

This sensor can also be used to irrigate plants. This sensor gives us the values of temperature and humidity around the plants to get idea about the weather in our field.

Camera:

This is used to take the photographs of the plants and leafs of the plants. These pics can be sent to farmer using Gpsmodule (or) can be stored. By seeing these pics one can say wetherthe plant is healthy or not. If not then the plants are given fertilizers
